About

developer | teacher | manager

Developer

Elissa is experienced with game, mobile, and web development. With over 8 years of experience, she began her career at Electronic Arts shipping games such as Fight Night 4, The Sims, and Need for Speed. In late 2011, Elissa worked as the CTO of MNE Creations Ltd, a mobile educational games startup in Shanghai, China. Her team was selected for Microsoft AppCampus to create a Windows Phone 8 game. Their team also shipped games for iOS and Android.

Teacher

During Elissa's time at Microsoft China, she worked as a technology evangelist. She wore many different hats including teaching & speaking at events for topics in game development, Unity3D, HTML5, and project management. She worked closer with the developer community and startups at Microsoft Game Labs. Elissa has spent 4 years in China volunteering for different organizations. She taught children in topics ranging from English, principles, and art.

Manager

Elissa manages projects, customers, and partner relationships. She is able to juggle between priorities for internal teams, vendors, and customers. Her recent project include a multi-million Microsoft Azure cloud deal for UCloudWorld gaming platform. She is experienced with scrum and agile principles throughout her projects. She is able to research and draft up proposals for the business needs.
